Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home Brownies Harder Cookie Recipe

Why These Cookies Are the Best

They are soft, simple, and perfect for beginners.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Mix sugar and butter.
  2. Add eggs.
  3. Add flour.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F for 12 minutes.
