Why These Brownies Are the Best

Rich, chocolatey, and super easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour

Instructions

  1. Melt butter.
  2. Mix in sugar and eggs.
  3. Add cocoa and flour.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 minutes.
