Advanced Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup melted chocolate
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup flour

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate and butter.
  2. Mix in sugar and eggs.
  3. Add flour.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes.
