Browned Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup browned butter
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Brown the butter in a pan.
  2. Combine sugar and browned butter.
  3. Add eggs.
  4. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F for 11 minutes.
